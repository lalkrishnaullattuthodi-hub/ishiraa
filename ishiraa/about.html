Our Story

A slower wardrobe, built with care.

Ishiraa began with a simple wish — to design clothing that felt like heirloom pieces, not fast trends.

Craft, first.

Each Ishiraa piece begins at a loom — sometimes in Benaras, sometimes in a quiet village in Madhya Pradesh. We work closely with master weavers, paying fair wages and giving the craft the time it deserves. The fabrics are then cut, stitched, and finished in our Bengaluru studio.

We only work in small batches. If a style sells out, it often means waiting a few weeks while the next run comes off the loom. We think that's worth it.

Natural fabrics.

Chanderi silk, handloom linen, mulmul cotton, khadi, tussar — each chosen for how it drapes, breathes, and ages. We avoid synthetics wherever possible and share the exact fabric composition on every product.

Our dyeing partners use low-impact and natural dyes. Pieces will fade over the years the way linen shirts and old jeans do — gracefully.

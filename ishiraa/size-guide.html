How to measure

Bust — around the fullest part of the bust, keeping the tape level.

Waist — the narrowest point of your torso, usually just above the navel.

Hip — the fullest part of your hips, about 7–9" below the waist.

Shoulder — from one shoulder seam to the other, across the back.

Tip: If you're between sizes, size up for tunics and kurtas; size down for fitted silhouettes like the anarkali.
